GEM CANDLE

A candle is an ignitable wick embedded in wax, or another flammable solid
substance such as tallow, that provides light, and in some cases, a fragrance.
A candle can also provide heat or a method of keeping time. A person who makes
candles is traditionally known as a chandler.
